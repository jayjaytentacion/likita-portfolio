Activepieces

Activepieces is an online no-code automation tool that connects your favorite apps, such as Gmail, Slack, Discord, and more. It allows you to automate repetitive tasks by creating workflows, called “Pieces”. A Piece connects two or more apps to automate part of your business or personal tasks without any coding or technical knowledge. This tool is used to streamline workflows and increase productivity by automating various processes.

During my time at Activepieces as a Product Designer I had the opportunity to contribute to the design projects aimed at improving user experiences. I worked closely with the Founder and Senior Designer to make complex processes more straightforward and ensure an integration, for our users. By following user centered design principles and implementing Material Design System Kit it fostered communication between designers and developers. One of my responsibilities was designing appealing interfaces for essential features such as the builder and dashboard which greatly contributed to Activepieces mission of simplifying automation, for users worldwide.

Redesigning Features

The Builder

The redesign of the Builder interface focused on creating a more intuitive and user-friendly experience. By simplifying the layout and incorporating clear visual cues, users can easily navigate through the workflow creation process. The improved design streamlines the selection of triggers and actions, making it easier for users to automate their tasks without any complications.

Select Trigger

The "Select Trigger" function has been improved to make it simpler and more accurate. Users can now easily identify trigger events using a search and filter system, ensuring a more precise selection. Clear explanations and visual cues have been added to help users understand the process intuitively. Real-time feedback during trigger selection boosts user confidence. With this user-friendly experience, the "Select Trigger" feature speeds up workflow creation, allowing users to effortlessly integrate apps and focus on their tasks without any complications.

Side Bar Apps

While redesigning the sidebar apps interface my main focus was, on making it clear and efficient. We presented the apps in a way to help users easily locate and incorporate the tools they require. User friendly icons and tooltips provide understanding of each app making them more accessible. We also implemented a drag and drop feature that enables integration, between apps ensuring an experience. By simplifying the process of selecting apps users can seamlessly integrate them and enjoy a user friendly journey.

Dashboard

I crafted an intuitive Dashboard interface that empowers users to effortlessly monitor their integrated data. Clear, visually engaging widgets provide real-time insights into app activities and automation statuses. Users can easily track and analyze their integrated data records, ensuring transparency and control. Interactive charts and customizable data visualizations enhance user understanding, enabling informed decision-making. The Dashboard's user-centric design promotes a seamless experience, allowing users to efficiently manage their integrated data, fostering a sense of control and confidence in their workflows.

Material Design System

The Material Design kit was utilized to enhance collaboration between designers and developers. It facilitated the use of consistent UI components, typography, and color schemes, establishing a cohesive visual language. Comprehensive documentation and reusable elements ensured design consistency across the application. By providing a shared reference point, designers effectively conveyed their vision, while developers accurately translated it into the final product. This seamless integration expedited development and yielded a visually appealing user interface that improved user experience and satisfaction.

Review

The appreciation not only validates the hard work I put into crafting intuitive interfaces but also motivates me to continue striving for excellence.
