0.1 OVERVIEW

RONDA

A Fintech solution. An app that offers disposable virtual card for swift online transaction and enable users deposit cheques from their homes.

This project aims at modifying banking experience for users by enabling them deposit cheques and virtual card access without visiting the banking hall.

This collaboration with friends on Design Pal focuses on answering the question of how might we improve the cheque banking system for users and how might we ensure users make a swift online transaction. Our primary users are banks users and business owners.

0.4 RESEARCH INSIGHTS

In order to create a solution that solves ‘real life’ problems, research was a key part of our design journey as it helped us get insight from potential users as well as ideas and opportunities to expoit going foward. The primary tested and trusted method for carrying out user research is the qualitative research method. In this project;

  • We carried out qualitative research which included one on one interviews and focus groups. Our aim was to have the interviewees answer open ended questions based on their motivations, pain points, goals and their previous experience; especially with the type of solution we were aiming to create so as to know what will work best for them.

How often do you get Cheques?

What is the resisting factor to cash out Cheques immediately?

How often do you need a disposable virtual card during online transactions?

What challenges do you face before you cash out Cheques?

Unavailability of In-App feature to cash out cheques

Visiting the Bank to get physical card and as such affects user during online shopping.

Long queue to process a cheque deposit

Unavailability of virtual and disposable virtual cards to act as extra layer of security for online payments.

14. LESSONS LEARNED

One important feature of human lives is the ability to do work, earn money, and depending on their choice, spend the accured funds or save them for later and banks will continue to play a paramount functional role to the smooth running of this process. In order to carry out this task effectively, they have to provide an entailing experience for the user to achieve their goals with little to no obstacles. If given primary focus, these important points can help produce these results:

  • A simplified interface with a proper navigation pattern. This involves considering alternate paths a user can take while exploring the solution.
  • Provision of adequate support and help when needed to assist users while interacting with the solution.
  • Implementing design decisions based on the conducted research and ensuring that as much as possible, each aspect of the solution is cross checked and validated by appropriate research.
