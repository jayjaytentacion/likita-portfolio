User Background

Shanaya is a caring and active pet owner who also leads a busy life. She is tech-savvy and heavily relies on her phone for various activities.

Goal

The objective is to establish a user-centric platform designed to enable Shanaya (Case-Study User) in finding and booking various fun activities and events for her pet, Cookie.

Browse Pets

The "Browse Pets Search Feature" allows users to easily search and explore available pets for adoption or purchase. It provides a user-friendly interface where individuals can filter and view pets based on criteria like species, breed, age, and location, streamlining the process of finding their ideal pet companion.

Events

This feature displays upcoming pet-related gatherings, activities, or important dates. Users can access event details, including dates, locations, and descriptions, allowing them to stay informed about and potentially participate in relevant pet-related events, such as adoption drives, pet shows, or fundraisers.

Favorite

This is a feature that enables users to mark and save their preferred pets or listings. It provides a convenient way for users to create a personalized list of pets they are interested in or considering for adoption, making it easier to revisit and compare their choices later.

Confirmation

"Confirmation after scheduling events" is a pop-up that provides users with a notification or message confirming that their event, such as a pet adoption appointment or a pet-related activity, has been successfully scheduled. This reassures users that their request or booking has been recorded and helps them keep track of their upcoming commitments in the pet-related context.

User Research

I conducted user research and gathered valuable feedback from individuals, particularly pet owners and enthusiasts. This feedback was used to shape a user persona, exemplified by Shanaya. For instance, Shanaya's frustration stems from her dog's limited social interactions due to her busy work schedule and the challenge of finding a platform where she can make informed choices. Her deep affection for her pet drives her desire to provide a more enjoyable experience.
