Project Brief

I held the pivotal role of Lead Product Designer within a startup, spearheading the complete redesign of TripDoodler. My expertise and creative direction played a key role in transforming the user experience and visual identity of the platform, resulting in a significant impact on its success.

Covering The User Journey

My first task was to complete improved architecture by synthesizing learnings, mapping out the user journey, and delivering site specifications and recommended site map. Two of the largest pain points were:

  • Non-qualified leads requesting a demo.
  • General confusion about what TripDoodler does and how.
    • I collaborated closely with TripDoodler's founding team to gain a deep understanding of the diverse user personas that the platform aimed to serve. My efforts involved meticulous analysis to identify any underserved segments within the user base. The resulting insights led to the identification of three primary user groups: Travelers seeking sustainable options in the free directory, individual adventurers looking to explore new destinations, and investors and travel organizations eager to discover sustainable destinations for their clientele. The current challenge surrounded the balance between currently being most known as a free resource, but also communicating the key product offering. From a long-term business perspective, we identified which user type best suits overall product revenue goals, in order to begin prioritizing them and their journey in the overall site structure.

    Notable solutions

  • Recognizing that the "Request A Demo" feature could deter users accustomed to straightforward sign-ups and transparent pricing, we streamlined the journey for non-enterprise customers, offering a more accessible path to explore and make sustainable travel choices. This involved the introduction of Sustainable Detail pages in place of a single overview page.
  • In the website's header, a crucial navigational tool, we strategically added a "Business" option, leading to a dedicated landing page showcasing expanded sustainability offerings.
  • Additionally, we enhanced the right navigation, providing a clear and user-friendly experience for those needing to log in, sign up for the TripDoodler app, or access profile information. This functions previously relegated to sub-domains and email links. These solutions were expertly crafted to improve user satisfaction and engagement.

    • Of course, our immediate focus was on seamlessly incorporating the refreshed visual identity. To align harmoniously with the modern style, I took proactive steps by crafting prototypes within Figma. These prototypes served as detailed guidelines for desired interactions and animations. Furthermore, I developed a comprehensive web design system, encompassing all colors, design elements, and states used in the design. This approach was instrumental in ensuring a unified and visually appealing user experience.
